Trigonometry Lesson

📘Definition

Trigonometry studies the relationships between the angles and sides of triangles, primarily right-angled triangles.

📘Basic Trigonometry Ratios

For a right-angled triangle with angleθ:
- Sinθ = opposite/hypotenuse
- Cosθ = adjacent/hypotenuse
- Tanθ = opposite/adjacent

📘Reciprocal Ratios

For a right-angled triangle with angleθ:
- Cosecθ = 1/sinθ
- Secantθ = 1/cosθ
- Cotangent = 1/tanθ
