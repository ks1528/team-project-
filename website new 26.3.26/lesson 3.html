Addition with intgers

1. Indentify the place values.

2. Place the larger number on top of the smaller one and ensure everything is inline.

3. Then multiply the values that are inline with one another and carry the exra over next value sum.

For example

    1 2 3
  × 4 5 6
  --------
      7 3 8   (123 × 6)
    6 1 5     (123 × 5, shifted one place)
+ 4 9 2       (123 × 4, shifted two places)
  --------
    5 6 0 8 8
