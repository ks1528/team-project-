Addition with intgers

1. Indentify the place values.

2. Place the larger number on top of the smaller one and ensure everything is inline.

3. Then subtract the values that are inline with one another to get your final result

4. If a value can not be subtracted by the number below, carry it on to the next unit value and continue with the subtraction

For example

  4 12 12
  5  3  2
- 1  7  8
---------
  3  5  4

  Now subtract:
12−8=4
12−7=5
4−1=3
