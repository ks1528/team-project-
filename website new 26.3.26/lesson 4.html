Division with intgers

1. Indentify the place values.

2. Place the larger number within the busstop method and the smaller number outdide

3. Then see if the smaller number can go into the first value of the biger number and carry the value over if there is a remainder and carry on until the bigger number is completed.

For example

     1 5 4
    --------
7 ⟌ 1 0 8 2
      7
      ----
        3 8
        3 5
        ----
          3 2
          2 8
          ----
            4
            7 goes into 10 → 1 (remainder 3)

            Bring down 8 → 38 ÷ 7 → 5 (remainder 3)

            Bring down 2 → 32 ÷ 7 → 4 (remainder 4)
