Welcome to the Year 7–8 Maths Hub!

This hub is designed for students in Year 7–8. Explore lessons, then test your skills in quizzes.

  • Adding, subtracting, and simplifying fractions

Lessons

Lesson 1: Ratios

A ratio compares two quantities. Example: 2 apples and 4 oranges is 2:4. Simplify by dividing both numbers.

Lesson 2: Fractions

Fractions are parts of a whole. Example: 1/2 + 1/2 = 1. Remember to find a common denominator if needed.

Lesson 3: Decimals

Decimals are another way to write fractions. Example: 0.5 = 1/2, 0.25 = 1/4.

Lesson 4: BIDMAS

BIDMAS = Brackets, Indices, Division, Multiplication, Addition, Subtraction. Follow this order in calculations.
